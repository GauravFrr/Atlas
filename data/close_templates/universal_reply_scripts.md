# GOLDEN RULES (Read These Every Day)

1. Reply within 1–2 hours whenever possible — speed kills in sales
2. Never send a price before asking qualifying questions first
3. Always end your reply with a question — keeps the conversation alive
4. Keep every reply under 8 lines — if it's longer, cut it down
5. Sound like a human, not a salesperson — casual and helpful wins every time
6. Never chase more than 3 times after a positive reply — respect their time
7. When they say yes — move fast, get the Fiverr link out same day
